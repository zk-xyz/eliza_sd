Welcome to Sober Dad

A community dedicated to supporting parents in recovery as they navigate the challenges and joys of raising children while maintaining sobriety.

How Can We Help?

Parenting is challenging enough on its own. Adding recovery to the mix can sometimes feel overwhelming. That's why we've created resources specifically for parents in recovery.

Our community offers:

  • Support groups specifically for parents
  • Resources for explaining recovery to children
  • Strategies for managing stress without substances
  • Tips for building healthy family dynamics
  • Guidance for navigating social situations

Whether you're newly sober or have years of recovery, we're here to support your journey as a parent.
